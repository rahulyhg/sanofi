About iLead

i Lead is a unique incentive program, which will honor your efforts at the market place for Sales, SFKPI & Processes through an exciting reward system.

i Lead is a point based program, in which you will accumulate reward points on achievement of pre-defined KPIs, which can be redeemed later for gifts through this Mobile Application or Web portal.

    How does it work?

  • The reward points are based on the consistent delivery of Pre-defined KPIs.
  • You will accumulate reward points on achievement of pre-decided KPI parameters, which can be redeemed at the end of the quarter after fulfilling the qualifying criteria.
  • You may choose to club the point for more than one quarter and redeem when you wish to, provided the qualifying criteria for redemption is met.
  • You can log on this Mobile application for the following-
    • To view your KPI performance
    • To check the points accumulated
    • To browse through the gift catalogue, to choose the gifts based on the points accumulated.
