Terms and Conditions

  • Employee will be entitled for points after fulfilling all of the below mentioned conditions:
    • the Quarter CPA (A+B) is more than or equal to 90%
    • the Quarter average of Calls Per Day is more than or equal to the BU’s Calls / Day Objective
    • the Quarter Average DRC is less than 3.0
  • Employee can accumulate the points for more than one Quarter. It can be redeemed at any time of the year or at the end of the year. Redemption shall be based on quarterly scores only.
  • To redeem the points, the Target achievement of the respective Quarter needs to be more than or equal to 90%.
    • In case the Quarterly Target Achievement is less than 90%, employee cannot redeem the points.
  • Employee can redeem the accumulated points of consecutive Quarters on achievement of more than or equal to 90% of the YTD Target
    • For example - If an employee had achieved less than 90% for first Quarter but the achievement is more than or equal to 90% for YTD at the end of 2nd Quarter, the employee can redeem the accumulated points of 1st & 2nd Quarters.
  • In case of resignation of the employee, the employee will not be entitled for any points in the current Quarter. He/She cannot redeem any of the accumulated points of previous Quarters as well, after resignation.
  • For new employee, employee will be entitled for points from the next Quarter after the joining.
    • For example - If the employee joins in the month of February, the employee will be entitled for points from the second quarter of 2016.
  • In case of transfer of employee, the points as per the actual achievement in the respective headquarters will be considered.
    • In case of transfer in the second month of the quarter, the achievement for the first month in the previous headquarters and the second and third months in the new head quarter will be considered.
  • In case of promotion of the employee in the mid of the Quarter, the employee will not be entitled for any score for the Quarter.
  • In case of promotion of the employee in the first month of the Quarter, the employee will be entitled for the points of the new position.
  • There will not be any exceptions considered for the entire program and will be independent of the all the other exceptions considered in the monetary incentive scheme.
